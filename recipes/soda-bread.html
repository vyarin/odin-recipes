Soda Bread

Description

An easy to make, traditional bread from Ireland.

Ingredients

  1. 3 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 tablespoon baking powder
  3. 1/3 cup white sugar
  4. 1 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  6. 1 egg, lightly beaten
  7. 2 cups buttermilk
  8. 1/4 cup butter, melted
